Celebrated in Collaboration with Google’s Women in Tech and SmartEdge at the Eighth Edition of GLOBALSPIN INNOVATION SUMMIT (By Invitation Only)

The 8th edition of GlobalSpin Innovation Summit is geared towards bringing together all stakeholders connected with the SME industry to deliberate and engage on how to take the sector forward, to enable global market access, efficiencies and growth in the MSME sector through Innovation and Technology.

SmartEdge is an integrated platform for Innovators. We aim to bring diverse stakeholders together to create a positive impact on the people, the planet and the society. The way we do it is by providing a conducive environment to learn, innovate and accelerate ideas, products and businesses.

Our main focus is to prepare women and youth to transform the way they work by acquiring new age skills and knowledge to manage the future of work. The main themes that we are currently working on are - Sustainability, Inclusion and Governance.

Women Tech Makers (WTM) are building a world where all women can thrive in tech. Google’s Women Tech makers program provides visibility, community, and resources for women in technology. Google provides support to the WTM community through WTM ambassadors, who are cherry picked to further the cause across the globe.

Since 2013, Women Tech makers Ambassadors have held thousands of global tech events, inspiring and connecting women in tech. There are 1,100 WTM Ambassadors as of date and growing, who have created and organised 605 events till date in 75+ countries.
